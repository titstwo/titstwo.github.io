Instructions

  1. Download the app Trust Wallet
  2. Purchase BNB or BSC
  3. Go to the DApps tab at the bottom of TrustWallet and find PancakeSwap. iPhone users may need to enable the Trust Browser then use the browser tab.

    To enable it on iPhone, open the Safari Browser and then type in the URL: trust://browser_enable then tap on Go. A prompt will appear that will ask if you want to Open this page in "Trust?", tap on Open.

    You can also click the BUY NOW button on our website if you're not on mobile.
  4. Click "Select a currency" and enter this contract address into the search field, and you should be able to find Tits Coin. 0xA753D241fFcE10A3570eDa72f6561C04b1f9Ae77
  5. Before swapping, click on the cog wheel and set the Slippage to between 11-12%
  6. Now set the amount you want to purchase and press the Swap button.
  7. Confirm the transaction and... Hold To End Breast Cancer
